Getting Started

How to Navigate

Use the sidebar menu to access different sections of the platform. The home page shows your dashboard with recent activities and important announcements.

Profile Setup

Complete your profile by adding your academic details, skills, and work experience. A complete profile increases your visibility to potential employers.

Placement Process

Application Process

Browse available opportunities in the Companies section. Apply to positions that match your skills and interests. Keep track of your applications in your dashboard.

Interview Preparation

Access resources and previous interview experiences in the Resources section. Stay updated with company-specific preparation materials.

Common Issues

Technical Support

For technical issues like login problems or profile updates, contact the technical support team through the support portal.

Placement Related Queries

For queries about placement processes, eligibility, or company-specific questions, reach out to your placement coordinator.
